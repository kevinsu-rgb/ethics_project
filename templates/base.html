Ethical Use of AI with Art

Kartik - Domenica - Brian - Kevin

Problem Statement

Artists' works are being used to train AI models to create art; however, this is generally done without the artists' permission, violating the artists' right to intellectual property.

Our Solution Statement

We aim to address the issue of AI tools violating the right to intellectual property of artists by creating this web platform. This web platform will enable artists to upload their content to one of our databases.

  • AI scientists can analyze the infringement of intellectual property with this dataset.
  • This dataset can be used to train models so that ethical AI development is ensured.
